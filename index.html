Bot commands

BeamRP uses a Discord bot for commands, the following are the commands you can use in #bot-commands


/initialize

Used for creating a new character

Arguments:

None

Examples:

/initialize
Removes all your money and resets your drivers license

/status

Displays your characterâ€™s stats, such as money and whether you have a driver's license

Arguments:

User: See the status of another user's character

Examples:

/status
Returns your balance, whether you have a license, and if you are blacklisted

/status @itsaname
Returns the balance, license status, and blacklist status, of @itsaname

/delmoney

Removes money from your character, used for purchases

Arguments:

Amount: The amount of money to remove

Reason: The reason to remove money

Examples:

/delmoney 6000 Bought ETK K-Series KC4 (M)
Removes 6000, lets the bot know you spent your money on buying an ETK

/delmoney 15000 Paying fines
Removes the money you spent on paying fines

/delmoney 700 Paid @itsaname
Removes the money you paid @itsaname (Doesn't automatically pay them)

/addmoney

Gives your character money

Arguments:

Amount: The amount of money to add

Reason: The reason to add money

Examples:

/addmoney 200 Two deliveries
Gives you $100 for doing two level 1 deliveries

/addmoney 700 Got paid by @gitty
Adds money that you were given by @gitty
